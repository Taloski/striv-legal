Welcome to Striv. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data we collect. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address: Required for authentication via Supabase Auth
  • Password: Stored encrypted by Supabase Auth - we never see your plain text password

Fitness Data

  • Workout sessions: Exercises performed, sets, reps, weights lifted, rest times between sets
  • Body measurements: Weight (in kg), body fat percentage
  • Exercise preferences: Favorite exercises, custom exercise configurations
  • Training routines: Custom workout programs you create
  • Workout schedules: Planned workout sessions and timing

Financial Information

  • Subscription purchase history: Processed by RevenueCat
  • Subscription status and entitlements: Whether you have premium access, trial status
  • Important: Payment methods are handled directly by Google Play. We never store credit card details or other payment information.

Device Information

  • Notification preferences: Used for locally scheduled weigh-in reminder notifications

App Activity

  • Exercise library interactions: Exercises you favorite or frequently use
  • Routine creation and configuration: Custom workout templates you build
  • Program schedules: Your training plans and calendar
  • User settings: Weight unit preferences (kg/lbs), calendar settings, rest timer defaults

2. How We Use Your Data

Core App Functionality

  • Sync your workouts across all your devices
  • Track your fitness progress over time
  • Calculate personal records (PRs) and volume PRs
  • Display workout history and analytics
  • Provide in-workout intelligence (previous performance, recent averages)

Notifications

  • Weigh-in reminders: Optional locally scheduled reminders you can configure or disable at any time

Subscription Management

  • Verify your premium feature access
  • Process subscription status and entitlements
  • Manage trial periods and grace periods

App Improvement

  • We may analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve features (no individual tracking)
  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Optimize app performance

3. Data Sharing

We share your data with the following third-party service providers who help us operate Striv:

Supabase (supabase.com)

  • Purpose: Database hosting, user authentication, data synchronization
  • Data shared: All user data including account information, fitness data, and app activity
  • Location: Data stored in Supabase cloud infrastructure

RevenueCat (revenuecat.com)

  • Purpose: Subscription management and in-app purchase processing
  • Data shared: Purchase history, subscription status, user ID, email address

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your data is only shared with the service providers listed above for the specific purposes of operating Striv.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transferred between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Database access control: Access to your data is restricted by Row Level Security (RLS) policies that ensure users can only access their own data
  • Secure authentication: Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using platform-standard secure storage
  • Password protection: Your password is never stored in plain text - it is encrypted by Supabase Auth

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access

You can view all your data within the app at any time. Your workout history, body measurements, routines, and settings are always accessible through the app interface.

Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by going to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Export

If you would like to export your data in a machine-readable format, please contact us at support@aesirtech.com.au. We will provide your data within 30 days of your request.

Correction

You can edit your data directly within the app at any time. This includes modifying past workout entries, updating body measurements, and changing your settings.

6. Data Retention

Active Accounts

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. This allows you to access your complete workout history and track your long-term progress.

Deleted Accounts

When you delete your account, all your personal data is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days. This includes:

  • Account information (email, authentication credentials)
  • All fitness data (workouts, body measurements, routines)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Subscription history (except as required for financial record-keeping)

7. Children's Privacy

Striv is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information as quickly as possible.

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at support@aesirtech.com.au.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make material changes, we will notify you by:

  • Sending an email notification to the address associated with your account
  • Displaying an in-app notice when you next open Striv
  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy

Your continued use of Striv after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
